About Me

With a unique blend of medical training, banking expertise, and academic leadership, I bring a multifaceted approach to executive coaching and business transformation. My journey from healthcare to finance, and ultimately to education and entrepreneurship, has equipped me with deep insights into leadership, strategy, and innovation.

As VP, Academic Director & COO at Nova Business School and Co-Founder of Venco Africa, I'm passionate about developing leaders who can navigate complexity and drive meaningful change. My work spans continents, touching the lives of executives, entrepreneurs, and students across 80+ countries.

Through personalized coaching, innovative educational programs, and entrepreneurial ventures, I help leaders unlock their potential and create lasting impact in their organizations and communities.

Author's Note

"Writing has always been my way of crystallizing the lessons learned from each chapter of my journey. From the precision required in medical practice to the strategic thinking demanded in banking, from the innovation mindset needed in entrepreneurship to the wisdom required for effective coaching—each experience has shaped my perspective on leadership and success.

I write not just to share knowledge, but to create practical tools that leaders can use immediately. Every framework, every case study, every insight in my books comes from real-world application. My hope is that these works serve as both mirror and map—helping you see your current capabilities clearly while charting a path to even greater impact.

Leadership is not a destination; it's a continuous journey of growth, adaptation, and service to others. Through these books, I invite you to join me on that journey."

— Dr. Anderson Uvie-Emegbo
